## About

I'm a sophomore at UNC Chapel Hill working at the boundary between **mechanistic modeling** and **deep learning** — I want to know *why* a model works, not just that it does.

My current research focuses on symbolic regression applied to Alzheimer's tau propagation, cardiac ultrasound segmentation, and incoming work on ML for optical blood flow imaging. I'm building toward a research career at the frontier of ML, with particular interest in **interpretable, physics-informed methods** for biomedical systems.
